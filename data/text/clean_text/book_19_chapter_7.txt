
Nt wo haichu emahgi ab amjed hegai Melkisedekbad mant wo emtashogi g Jesus chikpan
Jiosh atsh am i kei g Melkisedekbad
Kush d kownal am Salem kihhim ch ed ch d ep pahl
Kutsh idam Abrahambad gam hu hebai gidahiop k am hai ha kokda g pi apekam kokownal k ab uhpam hihim
Kutsh g Melkisedekbad ab ha nam k ab i shoigeel g Abrahambad am wehhejed g Jiosh
Tsh g Abrahambad ab mah mo haschu ab d wuikamaj g Jiosh mash d hemako tahpna mo wehstmahm e tahpanch g gehgewadaj
Melkisedekbad chehgig o hab d ahga Sapekam Kownal K g Salem hab d ahga Dodolimadag K heg hekaj d ep dodolimkam kownal
Pi o an hu hebai oohanas mas hedai d haamjedkam g Melkisedekbad ch has masma mahsi k has masma heb hu hih
K id am tchehgidch mat has masma in wo i wuhsh g Jiosh alidag ab dahm kahchim t amjed k am uhpam ep wo him k d wo uhgchu pahlk chum hekid
Melkisedekbad oki baich d i si shas haichu mo g Abrahambad
Tsh heg ab mah g hemako tahpna mo wehstmahm e tahpanch g gehgewadaj matsh am ui cheggiadag ed  K hab chuig g tchehanig matsh hegam twehm oodham mo d Levibad amjedkam ch d papal ab tamjed wo ui hegai hemako tahpna matsh wehstmahm wo e tahpanch g tgehgewadag
Kush ihda Melkisedekbad pi d Levibad amjedkam
Tsh eda g Abrahambad ab namkid hegai mo d Jiosh wuikam
Tsh g Melkisedekbad ab i shoigeel
Kuch smahch mat g geechu ab wo shoigeel g chumchu
K heg hekaj baich i geej g Melkisedekbad mo g Abrahambad
Melkisedekbad o baich d i si shas haichu mo g tpapalig
Hegam o wabsh koihim
K wabshaba pi an hu hebai oohanas mat muh g Melkisedekbad
K heg wa wepo mat g tpapalig hashohshonbad Levi am namkid g Melkisedekbad  nash pi ab d Abrahambad amjedkam mash heg am namkid
Jiosh atsh am i ha chuhcha g Levibad amjedkam matsh d wo tpapaligk ch ab wo tmah g chehanigaj
T wabshaba pi wo shai e nako g tpapalig mas am wo i tapech g Jiosh wehhejed
Tsh heg hekaj ab hema ep i wuhsh mash gawul masma mo hegam ch wepo masma g Melkisedekbad
Kut hemho wa am gawul juh g Jiosh g echehanig mat am wo i kei g Jesus mat d wo tpahligk
Heg ash pi ab hu d Levibad amjedkam ch wabshaba ab d Judabad amjedkam matsh idam pi d wo chum papalk am tchehanig ed
K heg hekaj tasho mat g Jiosh am gawul juh g echehanig am twehhejed
Neh baich ant am wo i emtashogi mo g Jesus pi ha wepo masma g tpapalig ch wabshaba wepo masma g Melkisedekbad
Pi atsh e pahlch g tchehanigkaj k wabshaba e pahlch g gewkdajkaj g edoakag
K g doakaj d pi ha huhugedam
Heg o hekaj ab amjed oohanas mash ab wui hab kaij g Jiosh 
Am ant i mkei hab masma mant wa am i kei g Melkisedekbad
Kupt chum hekid d wo uhgchu pahlk
Neh bo kaij g Jiosh am ealidag wui
Kut heg am i tapech Jiosh tahgio mach ab hahawa sap miabid
Heki hu tchehanig o pi ab hu shai tmahkch g gewkdag machs hekaj am wo i tapech Jiosh wehhejed
Kut hekaj g Jiosh gam hu hab i juh
Jiosh ash hab wabsh kaij matsh g Levibad amjedkam d wo papalk  ch wabshaba g egewkdagkaj hab kaij ab wui g Jesus 
Ahni ani g nigewkdagkaj hab si kaij mapt d wo uhgchu pahlk chum hekid
Kunt pi hekid am gawul wo juh g hab nielida
Neh bash kaij g Jiosh
K id am tchehgidch mat g Jiosh am i apech g baich i skehg apechuda mat hi heki hu nat pi am i kei g hejel ealidag mat d wo uhgchu pahlk chum hekid
Hegam Levibad amjedkam at pi wo e nako mas d wo papalk chum hekid no pi koihim
T wabshaba g Jesus chum hekid d wo doakamk
T hab pi hedai ep am kehkud ed wo i kekiwua
Kut wo e nako mat wehs wo ha doibia hegam mat am gahi wuhshanim ab wo i e nohnogi Jiosh wui nat pi chum hekid d wo doakamk ch am ha wehhejed ab wo tahnid g Jiosh mat am wo i ha wehmt
Tatchua ach g si sap uhgchu pahl
K g Jesus woho hab masma ch d hekia sapekam ch i e gawulkadch ab ha amjed g pi apekam
Idani o an daha Jiosh wehm an dahm kahchim ch ed
Pi o wepo masma hegam mo d uugchu papal am heki hu tchehanig ed
a Hegam o d pi ap chuijkam ch wehpeg ab wo haichu iagchul hejel echuijig wehhejed k ab haha wo iagchul g hemajkam hachuijig wehhejed
K wabshaba g Jesus pi ha chuijig
b Hegam o chum hekid ab haichu iagchulid Jiosh wui
T wabshaba g Jesus hemho hab juh
T heg wehs am i apech g pi ap hachuijig
c Hegam o ab haichu chum namkid g Jiosh
T wabshaba g Jesus hejel am i e dagito mat hekaj ab wo iagchul
Hegam mo d papal ab heki hu tchehanig wecho o pi d shai hekiakam ch pi ap wo nahto g echikpan
T wabshaba g Jiosh am hahawa i kei g ealidag g hejel egewkdagkaj
K heg d uhgchu tpahlig ch d si hekiakam
Pi at hekid wo huhug g gewkdaj
