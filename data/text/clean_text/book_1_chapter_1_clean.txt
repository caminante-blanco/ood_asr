
Ia atsh mahsi g Jesus Christ jewed dahm k ab d amjedkamaj hegai tkekelibad hakownalig mash hab chehgig David
Kush g Davidbad ab d amjedkamaj hegai mash d tshohshon ahchim Tutuligo ch hab chehgig Abraham
In ant wo ooha g hachechgig hegam mash d Jesus Christ shohshonbad
Abraham atsh wa an alidt g Isaac
Tsh g Isaac an alidt g Jacob
Tsh g Jacob an ha aalidt hegam Judah
Tsh g Judah an ha aalidt g Perez ch Zerah heg ab mash d Tamar
Tsh g Perez an alidt g Hezron
Tsh g Hezron an alidt g Ram
Tsh g Ram an alidt g Amminadab
Tsh g Amminadab an alidt g Nahshon
Tsh g Nahshon an alidt g Salmon
Tsh g Salmon an alidt g Boaz heg ab mash d Rahab
Tsh g Boaz an alidt g Obed heg ab mash d Ruth
Tsh Obed an alidt g Jesse
Tsh g Jesse an alidt g David
David atsh an alidt g Solomon heg ab mash d chum hohnigaj g Uriah
Tsh g Solomon an alidt g Rehoboam
Tsh g Rehoboam an alidt g Abijah
Tsh g Abijah an alidt g Asa
Tsh g Asa an alidt g Jehoshaphat
Tsh g Jehoshaphat an alidt g Joram

Tsh g Uzziah an alidt g Jodam
Tsh g Jodam an alidt g Ahaz
Tsh g Ahaz an alidt g Hezekiah
Tsh g Hezekiah an alidt g Manassah
Tsh g Manassah an alidt g Amos
Tsh g Amos an alidt g Josiah
Kush ihda Josiah d hashon hegam Jeconiah matsh eda gam hu hab i e juh g tshohshonbad Babylon kihhim wui
Jeconiah atsh an hahawa alidt g Shealtiel
Tsh g Shealtiel an alidt g Zerubbabel
Tsh g Zerubbabel an alidt g Abiud
Tsh g Abiud an alidt g Eliakim
Tsh g Eliakim an alidt g Azol
Tsh g Azol an alidt g Zadok
Tsh g Zadok an alidt g Akim
Tsh g Akim an alidt g Eliud
Tsh g Eliud an alidt g Eliazar
Tsh g Eliazar an alidt g Matthan
Tsh g Matthan an alidt g Jacob
Tsh g Jacob an alidt g Joseph
Kush ihda Joseph d kunaj hegai mo d Jesus jee ch hab chehgig Mary
K ihda Jesus hab e aaga Christ
Pegih nt gamai giik an ooha g hachechgig hegam mo d eoogbad am i amjed mash eda am oimmed g Abrahambad ch am hugkam mash eda am oimmed g Davidbad
Kunt hab waap gamai giik an ooha g hachechgig hegam mo d eoogbad heg i amjed ch gam hu hugkam matsh eda gam hu hab i e juh g tshohshon Babylon wui
Kunt gamai giik an ooha g hachechgig hegam mo d eoogbad heg i amjed ch gam hu hugkam matsh ab mahsi hegai mo hab e aaga Jiosh si Kehsha Christ
Ia ant wo ooha matsh has e juh matsh eda mahsi g Jesus Christ
Am amtsh i apech matsh idam Joseph ch Mary wo e hohont
Tsh am hahawa se mai mat g ali wo bei
K hab chuig g chehanig mat chum pi wo ha bei g cheoj matp hab wo wa e juh
Kush g Joseph am wabsh si sap oid g chehanig ch hekaj hab e elid mat pi wo be k wabshaba pi hedai wo ha ahgi
T hab pi wo si e el g Mary
Sh am oidahim ihda g Joseph
Kutsh am haha wabsh ab wui i e chehgi g anghil am chehchkij ed k hab kaij Joseph map ab d Davidbad amjedkam am g wabsh sap e tahtk k wo bei g Mary mat d wo mwehm kihkamk
